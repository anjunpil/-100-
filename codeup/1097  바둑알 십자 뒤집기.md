## 1097  바둑알 십자 뒤집기

```python
babuck=[[0 for _ in range(19)] for _ in range(19)]

for i in range(19):
  num=list(map(int,input().split()))
  babuck[i]=num
count=int(input())

for j in range(count):
  x,y=list(map(int,input().split()))
  for i in range(19):
    if baduck[i][x-1] ==1:
      baduck[i][x-1]=0
    else:
      baduck[i][x-1]=1
    if baduck[y-1][i]==1:
      baduck[y-1][i]=0
    else:
      baduck[y-1][i]=1
for k in range(19):
  print(*baduck)
```
