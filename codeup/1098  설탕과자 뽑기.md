## 1099  성실한 개미

```python
col,row=map(int,input().split())
zero=[[0 for _ in range(row)] for _ in range(col)]##
count=int(input())
for i in range(count):
  l,d,x,y = list(map(int,input().split()))
  if d ==0:
    for j in range(l):
      zero[x-1][y+j-1] =1
  else:
    for k in range(l):
      zero[x+k-1][y-1] =1

for m in range(col):
  print(*zero[m])
```
